Reflection

Taking CSIS 1430 has helped me better understand what web development is and what the process looks like to build a website from the ground up. I now understand how my browser renders websites to my screen and all the work that goes into building and designing them. Before taking this course, I did not have much knowledge of HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. I now feel comfortable with these topics and realize there is so much more to learn.

I now look at websites and use the developer tools to understand how popular blogs and other websites that I visit every day are structured. Knowing about these tools, the console, how to view properties and so much more has proven to be very useful when building my own websites in this course.

I like that web development brings creativity and logic together, you get the best of both worlds. I enjoyed being able to pick out color themes, fonts, and images that suited my style. I was challenged when it came to using position properties in CSS. Another challenging subject was JavaScript which allows you to make your website interactive which is what building websites is all about.

When learning the DOM (Document Object Model) you have to investigate properties to find out why things look the way they do or behave the way they do to then find out how to manipulate them and make them do the things you want. I learned the most from trying things out and seeing how it looked.

I am excited to keep learning about web development and using the skills I have obtained through this CSIS 1430 course.
